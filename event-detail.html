Current Series • Gala

Gala of The Voids

Mar 01 – Apr 30, 2024
11:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Main Exhibition Hall A

An Odyssey into Negative Space

The "Gala of the Voids" represents MOCA Art India's most ambitious exploration of architectural and mental negative space to date. For two months, the main exhibition halls are transformed into sensory-deprived chambers intended to provoke introspective dialogue between the viewer and the surrounding emptiness.

Featuring a curated selection of 12 international masters, the event challenges the conventional "museum block" by treating air, sound, and gravity as primary mediums. The opening gala night sets the tone with a multi-sensory performance that blends traditional subcontinental drone music with contemporary digital mapping.

"Space is not a vacuum; it is the most active participant in the artistic conversation." — Ar. Sameer Vohra, Curator.

Attendees will witness the unveiling of "The Silent Monolith," a 15-ton gravity-defying installation that serves as the center-piece of the Cohort 04 residency outcome. Private tours are available for institutional members every Wednesday evening.
