Our Heritage

Defining

MOCA Art India is more than a gallery. It is a sanctuary for modern thinkers, a platform for revolutionary creators, and a home for timeless masterpieces.

The Narrative

A Journey Through
Time and Vision

Founded on the principle that art should be both a historical anchor and a forward-looking catalyst, MOCA India began as a private collective of visionary patrons. Over decades, it has evolved into a centerpiece of Global-South modernism.

We pride ourselves on identifying the silent shifts in the art world, providing a stage for those whose voices are shaped by the intersection of traditional heritage and digital future.

Pillars of MOCA

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the world's most influential ecosystem for contemporary art from the subcontinent, fostering a global dialogue that transcends borders through the universal language of creative expression.

Our Mission

We are committed to curating high-impact exhibitions, supporting emerging creators through residency programs, and preserving artistic heritage through museum-grade archival practices.
